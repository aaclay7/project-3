Why do cats matter?

Cats are the most popular pet in the United States: There are 88 million pet cats and 74 million dogs. Although many cats have loving homes, many are not so lucky. Thousands of cats are abandoned and left for dead on the streets of busy cities, such as Los Angeles. The Department of Animal Services has picked up a numerous amount of strays off the streets in a matter of a year span. People must take responsiblity of domestic animals and take preventive measures to decrease the amount of stray cats.
