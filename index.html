Department of Animal Services Cat Intake 2013

By Alexandria Clayton


A closer look at the conditions of cats collected in Los Angeles, California


Why do cats matter?

Cats are the most popular pet in the United States: There are 88 million pet cats and 74 million dogs. Although many cats have loving homes, many are not so lucky. Thousands of cats are abandoned and left for dead on the streets of busy cities, such as Los Angeles. The Department of Animal Services has picked up a numerous amount of strays off the streets in a matter of a year. People must take responsiblity for domestic cats and take preventive measures, such as micro-chipping and neutering, to decrease the amount of stray cats. Unfortunately, most cats are euthanized after spending a specified amount of time at a shelter.
